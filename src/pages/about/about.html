About This ionic framework mobile app uses firebase's authentication and realtime database.

It stores song chords and lyrics for bands, allows users to view, edit, and delete songs, register bands, and invite other band members to see realtime changes in the songs and to filter songs by band name.

This app was made by Hao Nguyen (Laurea University of Applied Sciences, StudentID: 1601800) based on the tutorial by Daniel Hampikian on his YouTube channel.
